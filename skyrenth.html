The Problem

Your facility generates thousands
of security events daily.
Nobody reads them.

Physical security teams are flying blind — reactive, paper-heavy, sitting on a goldmine of behavioral data they've never analyzed.

01
Access Logs Nobody Reads
Every badge tap, failed entry, and after-hours access sits in a database security teams only open after something goes wrong. Zero proactive intelligence — only reactive forensics.
Avg. enterprise facility generates 10,000+ access events/day — 0% analyzed in real time.
02
Incident Reporting is WhatsApp
Guards report via text, paper, or radio. No structured data. No pattern detection. The same incident type occurs six times at the same location before anyone notices there's a pattern.
68% of recurring security incidents are caused by undetected patterns from prior incidents.
03
Guard Scheduling is Excel
Security managers build schedules by intuition and headcount. Patrol completion is self-reported. There's no way to verify a guard walked the perimeter or just sat at a checkpoint.
40% of guard time is misallocated relative to actual historical incident hotspots.
